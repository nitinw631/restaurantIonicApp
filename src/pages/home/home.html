We have been one of Montreal's leading Indian restaurants since 1991. As a pioneer of Indian cuisine in the Jean Talon(Parc-Extension) area of Montreal, we have a long tradition of serving authentic Indian food the way it is enjoyed in Indian homes. Our convenient location borders Le Plateau Mont Royal, Outremont and TMR (Town of Mount Royal).
